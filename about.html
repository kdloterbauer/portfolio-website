About Me

My Story

I received my BFA in Graphic Design in from Fort Lewis College in 2012. My Bachelors focused on branding, typography, advertising and web design. I also was the President of the AIGA Chapter in my school for one year. After receiving my bachelors, I followed a life of adventure as a Back Country Mountain Bike Guide, and after that as a Massage Therapist. In both of my previous fields I developed very strong interpersonal customer service skills - always centering around my clients needs and safety. I had to be resourceful in the back country and as a business owner to take care of my clients and provide an enjoyable experience for them. These experiences give me a deep understanding of the importance of designing user centric products, and my background in visual design create a strong foundation for graphic, and UI design to create successful and beautiful designs for my clients. I recently completed a Career Foundry course to specialize in UI design to get back into the world of design with more relevant experience. I love to work with my clients to curate unforgettable visual experiences for their users and future clients.
